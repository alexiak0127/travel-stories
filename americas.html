U.S.

  • Brooklyn Bridge

  • Like its title of one of New York's historic symbols, you can see a marvelous view of Manhattan on the bridge.

  • The Museum of Modern Art, NYC

  • If you are interested in art, don't forget to go to MoMa! MoMa holds a series of amazing exhibitions, being one of the most famous museums in the world.

  • NYC Covered in Snow

  • Snow adds more beauty to the city. If you want to see the city covered in snow, plan your trip in winter!

  • Time Square

  • Time Square is one of the most energetic places in the world. Watching Broadway shows would be one of the most exciting things to do in NYC!

  • Fifth Avenue Shopping District

  • Fifth Avenue's shopping district between 49th and 60th streets is one of the most lively shopping districts in the world. A number of world-famous stores are there, including Louis Vuitton, Chanel, and Saks Fifth Avenue.

  • New Museum, NYC

  • New Museum displays artworks of notable painters, such as Peter Saul. Don't forget to check it out if you are an art person!

  • Financial District

  • The Financial District in downtown Boston is the business hub of the city. You will see a number of offices there, forming a business vibe.

  • Boston University

  • Boston is famous for its academic strength, due to a number of top universities located near or in Boston. Don't forget to take a look at those world-famous schools!

  • The Seaport District

  • The Seaport District is one of the must-visit spots in Boston. The combination of the view of the sea and sleek bars and restaurants constantly attract a lot of people.
