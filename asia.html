ASIA

  • Goa

  • Goa is known as the Vegas of India. A popular tourist destination, visitors can enjoy Goa's beaches, casinos, and vibrant nightlife.

  • Hyderabad

  • Hyderabad is known as the City of Pearls and is famous for its traditional Indian dish Biryani, old Persian-style architecture, and beautiful scenery.

  • Pondicherry

  • Pondicherry is also known as French India. Originally occupied by the French, Pondicherry is famous for its architecture that resembles the streets of France.

  • Haebangchon Village

  • Haebangchon is a village located on the hill, in the middle of Seoul, and features rooftops with breathtaking views, amazing restaurants, and cute cafes.

  • Bukchon Hanok Village

  • As one of the most traditional and historical villages in Seoul, Bukchon Hanok Village is home to hundreds of Hanoks, the traditional Korean houses. The village showcases the culture of the Joseon Dynasty

  • Gana Art Center, Pyeongchang-dong

  • Pyeongchang-dong is well known for stylish art galleries. Don't forget to check out some contemporary art pieces!

  • Ikseon-dong

  • The maze-like streets in Ikseon-dong feature quaint shops and cafes converted from ancient architectures. If you want to see a perfect blend of modern and history in the heart of Seoul, Ikseon-dong is the move!

  • Insa-dong

  • Insa-dong is one of the most 'Korean' places in Seoul. Everything, including English names like Starbucks, is written in Korean. Insa-dong is filled with museums, shops, cafes, and restaurants.
