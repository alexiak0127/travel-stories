EUROPE

  • Galeries Lafayette Rooftop Terrace: A View over Paris

  • Some parts of the rooftop terrace were closed because of Covid-19, but the view of Paris, from the Tour Eiffel to Montmartre, was still breathtaking. With the combination of the sunset, it was amazing to see.

  • Tour Eiffel

  • Like its title of France’s most iconic monuments, the daring design of the Eiffel Tower and how marvelous it was were very impressive.

  • Le Louvre from the Outside

  • The Musée du Louvre was beautiful even from the outside. It is a perfect spot for those who love aesthetic, old European-style architecture and art.

  • La Pyramide du Louvre

  • The most famous pyramid of the Louvre is the one outside at the main entrance, but there are actually five pyramids throughout the museum! They are all beautiful, and this picture is the inverted pyramid that is visible from underground.

  • Le Louvre from the Inside

  • This is the picture of Galerie d'Apollon, which has the famous jewels of the French Crown Jewels and the Regent Diamond. The ceiling is also marvelous, showing the history.

  • Moulin Rouge

  • Close to Montmartre, the famous cabaret Moulin Rouge’s presence is iconic. One of the shows you should definitely watch when you visit Paris!

  • St. Pancras International Station

  • Eurostar would sound familiar if you have or are planning to travel to another European country by train. St. Pancras International Station is home to Eurostar and aesthetic shops, restaurants and bars.

  • Camden Town

  • Camden Town is a hub for London's live music scene and night-out places. Their food trucks are also amazing!

  • Traditional Red Phone Box

  • As one of the most familiar sights on the streets of London, seeing the red telephone booths will constantly make you feel like you are diving into the culture.

  • On The Way to Primrose Hill

  • Even the way to Primrose Hill, the hilltop part that is famous for its panoramic views, is beautiful. The sunset and double-decker buses boost its beauty.

  • In the Camden Market I

  • Even the instant photo booth machine is so aesthetic in London. Don’t forget to take a picture with your people when you visit Camden!

  • In the Camden Market II

  • Camden market is a hub for all the aesthetic shops and restaurants in London. A highly recommended place to buy souvenirs or visit on the weekend!

  • Oxford Street I

  • Oxford Street is definitely one of the places that really has London vibes. Go shopping in one of Europe's most aesthetic and busiest shopping streets!

  • Oxford Street II

  • Oxford Street’s night view with double-decker buses is amazing! Just walking around the street made me feel a lump in my throat.

  • Soho London

  • One of the most energetic streets in London, with a variety of options from nightlife, dining, and shopping!
